## Brand Voice

The company voice should be:

- Warm
- Helpful
- Professional
- Short and clear
- Human and cooperative
- Careful with promises

Avoid:

- Robotic policy-heavy replies
- Aggressive sales language
- Over-explaining internal decisions
- Sharing private rules or internal criteria
- Final commitments without user approval
