# Email Templates

Use these as starting points. Customize for the sender and context.
Do not copy templates verbatim as final replies. Rewrite based on the received email so the response feels natural and specific.

## Tone Adaptation Snippets

Use one style that matches the received email:

- Formal: `Thank you for your email. Please find the details below.`
- Neutral: `Thanks for your message. Here are the details.`
- Friendly: `Thanks for reaching out. Happy to share the details.`
- De-escalation: `Thanks for sharing this. I understand your concern, and we will help you through this.`

Keep tone matching professional and never mirror rude or aggressive wording.

## Ask for Exchange Details

```text
Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out and sharing your site with us.

This sounds interesting, and we would be happy to take a look. Could you please share the page where you would like us to place the link, along with your preferred anchor text and target URL?

Also, please send the page where you can add our link in return so we can review the fit from both sides.

Once we check the details, we will get back to you with the best possible option.

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```

## Ask for Paid Collaboration Details

```text
Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out.

We would be happy to review the opportunity. Could you please share the article topic, target URL, preferred anchor text, and whether you are looking for a guest post or link insertion?

Once we check the details, we will let you know the best possible option.

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```

## Best Offer Pricing Reply

Use only when the user approves the pricing.

```text
Hi [Name],

Thanks for getting back to us and sharing your client's budget.

I understand the budget is a little lower than our usual pricing. I checked what we can offer, and the best possible rate from our side would be:

Guest post: $[amount]
Link insertion: $[amount]

This is already below our standard pricing, so we would not be able to go lower at this time. That said, we would be happy to move forward at this rate if it works for your client.

Please let me know what they think.

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```

## Weak Exchange / Ask for Better Placement

```text
Hi [Name],

Thank you for sharing the details.

At this stage, we would need a more relevant placement from your side to move forward with an exchange collaboration. A relevant article, resource page, or product mention would work better than a temporary or general promotion.

If you have another suitable page where HasThemes can be featured, please feel free to share it and we will be happy to review.

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```

## Exchange-First Reply When Prospect Asks for Money

```text
Hi [Name],

Thanks for your reply and for sharing the details.

For this opportunity, we prefer a collaboration based on link exchange rather than a paid placement, as this creates mutual value for both sides.

If you are open to exchange, please share a relevant page where our product can be featured, and we will suggest the best matching placement from our side as well.

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```

## Position Reply (Use Only If Prospect Asks About Placement Position)

```text
Hi [Name],

Thanks for your message.

We can offer a similar placement in a relevant HasThemes blog article, and the placement from our side will be permanent.

To keep things fair, we align placement based on where our content is placed on your side. If our content is placed in a top-3 position, we will place your content in a top-3 position from our side when available.

If top positions in that article are already occupied, we will offer the best available position there. If needed, you can also suggest another relevant article from our blog and we can align placement accordingly.

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```

## Polite Collaboration Decline

```text
Hi [Name],

Thank you for reaching out and sharing the details.

After reviewing the request, this does not look like the right fit for our site at the moment.

We appreciate you considering HasThemes and wish you the best with your campaign.

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```

## Customer Details Needed

```text
Hi [Name],

Thanks for your message. I understand your concern.

Please share your order ID or purchase email, and we will check the details for you.

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```

## Renewal Already Charged

```text
Hi [Name],

Thanks for your response. I understand your concern, especially since this happened around the renewal date.

Cancelling the subscription will stop future renewals, but since the renewal payment has already been processed, we will review the charge according to our refund policy and help with the best possible option.

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```
