# Skill: Content Collaboration

## Purpose

Coordinate blog, guest article, content update, product mention, editorial review, and article improvement work.

## Shared Files To Use

Before drafting, use:

- `../GLOBAL_RULES.md`
- `../context/company-context.md`
- `../context/product-context.md`
- `../context/collaboration-rules.md` if placement, links, or exchange value are involved
- `../templates/email-templates.md` when a reusable reply fits

## When To Use

Use this skill for:

- Guest post topic discussion
- Blog collaboration
- Article update requests
- Product mention requests
- Editorial coordination
- Content brief creation
- Author or editor communication
- Blog publishing follow-ups

## Input Needed

Ask for or infer:

- Content goal
- Website or blog URL
- Topic or article URL
- Target product or page
- Target audience
- Deadline
- Author/editor requirements
- Content guidelines

## Workflow

1. Identify the content collaboration type.
2. Clarify topic, audience, target product, and placement.
3. Check the sender's tone and editorial style from their message.
4. Check whether the content fit is natural.
5. Suggest a topic, outline, brief, or reply.
6. Keep communication editorial and collaborative.
7. Avoid promotional overstatement.

## Tone Rules

- Editorial and cooperative
- Clear and practical
- Helpful without being pushy
- Focused on reader value
- Match the editor's communication style while staying professional
- Keep responses direct for transactional editors and warmer for collaborative conversations

## Do

- Suggest relevant topics.
- Ask for content guidelines when needed.
- Mention product value naturally.
- Keep article pitches specific.

## Don't

- Make the content sound like an advertisement.
- Promise publication or placement without approval.
- Stuff keywords unnaturally.
- Use unsupported product claims.

## Output Format

For a reply:

```text
Hi [Name],

[Content collaboration response]

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```

For a brief:

```text
Title:
Goal:
Audience:
Target product/page:
Suggested outline:
Notes:
```

## Example User Instruction

Create a reply to an editor asking us to submit guest post topic ideas.

