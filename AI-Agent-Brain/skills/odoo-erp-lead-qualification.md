# Skill: Odoo ERP Lead Qualification

## Purpose

Qualify Odoo ERP leads professionally and collect the information needed for sales or technical consultation.

## Shared Files To Use

Before drafting, use:

- `../GLOBAL_RULES.md`
- `../context/company-context.md`

## When To Use

Use this skill for:

- New Odoo ERP inquiries
- ERP implementation leads
- CRM, accounting, inventory, HR, POS, website, eCommerce, or manufacturing ERP questions
- Discovery emails
- Follow-up emails
- Sales handoff summaries

## Input Needed

Ask for or infer:

- Company name
- Contact person
- Business type
- Required Odoo modules
- Current system or pain point
- Number of users
- Country or timezone
- Implementation timeline
- Budget range if appropriate
- Preferred meeting time

## Workflow

1. Identify the lead's business need.
2. Ask concise discovery questions.
3. Avoid overwhelming the lead with too many questions.
4. Suggest a short meeting or consultation when suitable.
5. Prepare an internal qualification summary for the sales team.

## Tone Rules

- Professional and consultative
- Helpful and clear
- Not too technical unless the lead is technical
- Focused on business outcomes

## Do

- Ask about modules, users, timeline, and current challenges.
- Offer a meeting when the lead seems qualified.
- Summarize lead quality internally.
- Keep replies easy to answer.

## Don't

- Quote final pricing without discovery.
- Promise implementation timelines without technical review.
- Ask too many questions in one email.
- Use generic ERP jargon.

## Output Format

For lead reply:

```text
Hi [Name],

[Acknowledgement]

[2-5 discovery questions or meeting suggestion]

Best regards,
HasTech Team
```

For internal summary:

```text
Lead summary:
Company:
Need:
Modules:
Users:
Timeline:
Qualification level:
Recommended next step:
```

## Example User Instruction

Reply to an Odoo lead asking about inventory and accounting implementation.

## Example Final Output

```text
Hi Rahim,

Thanks for reaching out to us.

We can help you evaluate an Odoo setup for inventory and accounting. To understand the requirement better, could you please share:

1. Your business type
2. Approximate number of users
3. Whether you are currently using any accounting or inventory software
4. Your expected implementation timeline

Once we have these details, we can suggest the best next step and arrange a short discussion if needed.

Best regards,
HasTech Team
```
