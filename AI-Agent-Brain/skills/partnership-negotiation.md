# Skill: Partnership Negotiation

## Purpose

Handle partnership, sponsorship, guest post, affiliate, co-marketing, listing, and pricing negotiation conversations.

## Shared Files To Use

Before drafting or analyzing, use:

- `../GLOBAL_RULES.md`
- `../context/company-context.md`
- `../context/collaboration-rules.md`
- `../context/product-context.md` when product matching is needed
- `../templates/email-templates.md` when a reusable reply fits

## When To Use

Use this skill for:

- Paid guest post requests
- Paid link insertion requests
- Sponsorship proposals
- Affiliate partnership discussion
- Co-marketing offers
- Product listing proposals
- Partner page mention requests
- Pricing negotiation

## Input Needed

Ask for or infer:

- Collaboration type
- Partner website or company
- Offer or request
- Target URL and anchor text if links are involved
- Article topic or content draft if publishing is involved
- Placement page
- Budget or proposed pricing
- Approved minimum pricing if negotiation is requested

## Workflow

1. Classify the partnership type.
2. Identify whether it is paid, exchange-based, or unclear.
3. Read the sender's latest message for tone, urgency, and negotiation position.
4. Privately evaluate relevance, quality, risk, and brand fit using `collaboration-rules.md`.
5. Answer the sender's direct question first.
6. Ask for missing details before committing.
7. Negotiate politely if pricing is involved.
8. Do not finalize terms without user approval.
9. Keep final wording message-specific and natural; do not paste templates as final output.

## Tone Rules

- Cooperative and businesslike
- Flexible but not weak
- Friendly without sounding desperate
- Clear about what is needed next
- Match sender formality level while keeping brand-safe wording
- If sender is very brief, keep reply concise and direct
- If sender is price-sensitive, stay respectful and firm without sounding rigid

## Do

- Ask for topic, URL, anchor text, and placement details.
- Say "best possible rate" only when the user has approved the number.
- Keep negotiation concise.
- Protect HasThemes' brand value.

## Don't

- Reveal internal pricing logic.
- Mention banned niche lists.
- Promise publication before review.
- Guarantee do-follow links unless approved.
- Invent prices.
- Expose private review criteria, exchange ratios, or approval thresholds.
- Mirror pressure tactics, rude tone, or manipulative wording.

## Output Format

For analysis:

```text
Summary:
Partnership type:
Missing details:
Risk level:
Recommended action:
Draft reply:
```

For reply:

```text
Hi [Name],

[Reply]

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```

## Example User Instruction

Draft a reply to someone asking for our guest post and link insertion price, but they have not shared topic or URL.

## Example Final Output

```text
Hi Sarah,

Thanks for reaching out.

We would be happy to review the opportunity. Could you please share the article topic, target URL, preferred anchor text, and whether you are looking for a guest post or link insertion?

Once we check the details, we will let you know the best possible option.

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```
