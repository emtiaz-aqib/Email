# Skill: Email Outreach

## Purpose

Create professional outreach emails for prospects, partners, agencies, bloggers, affiliates, marketplaces, and product-related contacts.

## Shared Files To Use

Before drafting, use:

- `../GLOBAL_RULES.md`
- `../context/company-context.md`
- `../context/product-context.md` when positioning a HasThemes product
- `../templates/email-templates.md` when a reusable reply fits

## When To Use

Use this skill for:

- Cold outreach
- Warm follow-ups
- Product introduction emails
- Collaboration invitations
- Blogger or agency outreach
- Affiliate recruitment outreach
- Re-engagement emails

## Input Needed

Ask for or infer:

- Recipient name or company
- Recipient website or role
- Outreach goal
- Product or offer
- Reason the recipient is relevant
- Desired next step
- Any approved link, discount, or proposal

## Workflow

1. Identify the recipient and business fit.
2. Choose a short, relevant angle.
3. Write a natural subject line if needed.
4. If replying to an existing thread, mirror the recipient's tone and pace.
5. Open with a specific reason for reaching out.
6. Explain the value in one or two sentences.
7. Ask for a simple next step.
8. Keep the email polite and easy to reply to.
9. If replying to an existing email, rewrite based on the sender's exact message instead of using a fixed template.

## Tone Rules

- Friendly and confident
- Short and respectful
- Helpful, not pushy
- Personalized when information is available
- Match recipient formality level and keep phrasing natural
- Use a concise tone for short threads and fuller context for longer threads

## Do

- Mention a relevant reason for contacting them.
- Keep the ask simple.
- Use clear value language.
- Offer to share more details.

## Don't

- Overhype the product.
- Pretend a relationship exists.
- Use fake flattery.
- Make claims that are not provided or verified.
- Add too many links.

## Output Format

```text
Subject: [Short subject]

Hi [Name],

[Opening with reason for contact]

[Value or proposal]

[Simple next step]

Best regards,
HasThemes Team
```

## Example User Instruction

Write an outreach email to a WordPress blogger asking if they are open to reviewing ShopLentor.
